Poor UX

Critical UX Issues

🚨 Homepage is a Wall of Text

Dense paragraphs, no visual breaks. Users bounce within 5 seconds. Modern sites use hero sections, video, animations, and scannable content.

🚨 No Visual Hierarchy or Whitespace

Everything crammed together. Headings, body text, and CTAs blur together. Overwhelming and hard to parse.

🚨 Stock Photography Overload

Generic Wix template images. No custom graphics, team photos, or personality. Indistinguishable from competitors.

⚠️ Weak CTAs

"Get in Touch" is passive. Competitors use "Get Free Audit" or "Calculate Savings".

⚠️ No Trust Signals

No client logos, no quantified case studies. Generic testimonials don't convert skeptics.
