Technical SEO Issues

  • 🚨 No Sitemap Submitted to Google
    Wix auto-generates a sitemap, but verify it's submitted to Google Search Console. If not, Google may not be indexing all your pages.
  • ⚠️ Slow Mobile Page Speed
    Desktop is mediocre, but mobile is where 60%+ of B2B research happens. Run a Google PageSpeed Insights test—Wix sites typically score 20-40 on mobile. This hurts rankings and conversions.
  • ⚠️ Missing Robots.txt Optimization
    Wix's default robots.txt may be blocking important pages or allowing crawlers to waste time on admin URLs. Audit and optimize.
  • ℹ️ No Favicon Visible in Some Browsers
    Minor but professional: ensure favicon loads correctly across all browsers and bookmark scenarios.

Recommended Action Plan

🎯 Priority 1: Immediate Wins (0-30 Days)

  1. Fix homepage meta description – Add keyword-rich description (140-160 chars): "Spin LGX helps companies reduce logistics costs through expert contract optimization, freight auditing, and gainshare pricing. Save 20-40% on shipping."
  2. Update title tags – Every page should be "[Service] | Spin LGX" → "Parcel Contract Optimization | Spin LGX"

  4. Create a lead magnet – "Free LTL Rate Benchmarking Report" or "Parcel Contract Audit Checklist". Gate it with an email form.
  5. Launch a blog – Publish first 3 articles targeting high-value keywords: "How to Negotiate UPS Contracts", "LTL Freight Audit Best Practices", "Hidden Fees in Parcel Invoices".

🎯 Priority 2: Platform Migration (30-90 Days)

  1. Migrate off Wix to a modern, performant platform:
    • Next.js + Vercel – Best for SEO, speed, and scalability. Load times under 1 second.
    • Astro + Cloudflare Pages – Ultra-fast static site generator, perfect for content-heavy sites.
    • WordPress (Headless) – Familiar CMS with modern frontend. Good middle ground.
  2. Rebuild with SEO-first architecture – Semantic HTML, structured data, optimized images, lazy loading.
  3. Implement analytics properly – Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, heatmaps (Hotjar), and conversion tracking.

🎯 Priority 3: Content Machine (60-180 Days)

  1. Publish 2-4 blog posts per month targeting buyer-intent keywords: "freight audit services for [industry]", "parcel contract negotiation tips", "LTL shipping cost reduction strategies".
  2. Create service-specific landing pages – One page per service (6 total), each with unique content, case studies, CTAs.
  3. Develop case studies – Document 3-5 client success stories with real numbers: "Company X saved $180K in first year".
  4. Launch email newsletter – Bi-weekly industry insights, share blog content, nurture leads.
  5. Build backlinks – Guest post on logistics industry blogs, get listed in industry directories, sponsor relevant podcasts.

🎯 Priority 4: Conversion Optimization (90-180 Days)

  1. A/B test CTAs – Test "Get Free Audit" vs "Schedule Consultation" vs "Download Guide".
  2. Add live chat – B2B buyers want instant answers. Drift or Intercom can qualify leads 24/7.
  3. Build a cost savings calculator – Interactive tool: "How much could you save?" Input annual shipping spend, get estimated savings. Huge lead gen tool.
  4. Video content – Founder/exec explaining the gainshare model, client testimonial videos, service explainers.
  5. Retargeting campaigns – Use Google Ads and LinkedIn to retarget site visitors with blog content and case studies.
