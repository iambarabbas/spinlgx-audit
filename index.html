Executive Summary

Spin LGX is a logistics consulting firm built on Wix, offering gainshare-model contract optimization services. While the site communicates the core value proposition, it suffers from significant technical debt, poor SEO implementation, zero content marketing, and is falling behind more sophisticated competitors.

The Bottom Line: The site works, but it's not winning business. Competitors are outranking, outperforming, and out-educating Spin LGX with robust content strategies, faster sites, and stronger technical SEO.

Critical Issues & Business Impact:

▪ Wix Platform Bloat (662KB page size, slow load times)

Why it matters: 40% of visitors abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds to load. Every second of delay reduces conversions by 7%. Slow sites also rank lower in Google search results.

▪ Zero Content Marketing (no blog, no thought leadership)

Why it matters: B2B buyers conduct 70% of their research online before contacting a vendor. Without educational content, you're invisible during this critical research phase. Competitors with 40+ blog posts capture these early-stage leads.

▪ Poor SEO Foundation (missing meta descriptions, no schema markup)

Why it matters: 75% of users never scroll past the first page of search results. Without proper SEO, potential clients searching "logistics contract optimization" or "parcel freight audit" will find your competitors instead of you.

▪ Generic Design & Weak Conversion Elements

Why it matters: First impressions are formed in 50 milliseconds. A generic, dated design signals "small vendor" rather than "trusted partner." Without lead magnets and clear CTAs, visitors leave without converting — even if they're interested.
